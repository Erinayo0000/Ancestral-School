Upcoming Event

Inter-house Sport Competition

Inter-house competitions are designed to develop
students' mental and physical skills as well as help them
realize their potential and bring out the best in them. The
purpose of the inter-house competition is to encourage
students to participate in sports to combat the current
problem of stress and to make sure they are healthy and
fit.

Time and location

April 2023 by 10:am at Mimiko Abiola Sport Complex,
Adjacent Police Barracks in Surelere, Lagos State.

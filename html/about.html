About Us

Who We Are

Ancentral High School is a community of learners made
up of students, parents, and faculty who are committed to
fostering a challenging academic environment that is
also welcoming to all cultures. We are making history and
achieving academic excellence! We stand up for
knowledge, our neighborhoods, the environment, and our
destiny as a group.

Our Mission

We aim to create well-rounded children who are models
of health, wellbeing, and character development using
the fundamental standards and principles shared by the
entire school.

Our Values

The guiding principles of our school's values education
program are as follows:

  1. Acceptance
  2. Respect\Resilience\Responsibility
  3. Excellence
